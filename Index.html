Our Game Modes

Bedwars

In this competitive team-based game mode, your goal is to destroy other teams' beds while defending your own. If your bed is destroyed, you can no longer respawn. Strategize, gather resources, and fight to be the last team standing!

Survival

Experience classic Minecraft survival! Gather resources, craft tools, and build your world while fending off hostile mobs. There's no end goal hereâ€”just survive and thrive in a world of endless possibilities!

Earth SMP Faction

Join factions and fight for dominance in a world modeled after Earth! Claim land, create alliances, and battle for resources. Build your faction's empire while defending your territory from rivals.

Skywars

Battle other players in the sky! Players are placed on separate islands, and the goal is to be the last one standing. Gather resources, build bridges, and engage in aerial combat to eliminate your opponents!

Skyblock

Start with just a small island and a few resources. Your goal is to expand, survive, and complete challenges using limited items. Can you build an entire world from just one block?

Oneblock

Begin on a single block in the void! Each time you break the block, it regenerates into different materials. Use these resources wisely to build and expand your world. It's a test of creativity and survival!

Minigames

Play a variety of fun and fast-paced minigames. In Spleef, break blocks under players to make them fall. TNT Run challenges you to survive as blocks disappear beneath you. And there's much more to explore!
